Death Note

♡ One of the most timeless animes created ♡

Death Note is a Japanese manga series written by Tsugumi Ohba. The story begins with a teenager in Tokyo, named Light Yagami, who stumbles upon a mysterious book called “The Death Note”. This book grants the user a supernatural ability to kill any person whose name is written inside of it. The narrative follows Light Yagami in his fanatical attempt to utilize his recent discovery to create a crime-free society.
